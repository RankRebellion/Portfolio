Miniature Painting Museum

Itch.io

Miniature Painting Museum is a game made for GMTK Game Jam 2024

In this game the player paints a series of miniature models which can be viewed to their real scale in a museum environment. This project was a great learning opportunity for UI and audio design and complex material systems
